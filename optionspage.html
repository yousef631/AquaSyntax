How can you save water?

small actions today make a big difference tomorrow

Fix leaks

A dripping faucet or pipe can waste hundreds of liters over time. Repairing leaks saves a lot of water effortlessly.

Take shorter showers

Reducing shower time by just 2–3 minutes can save dozens of liters every day. It's one of the easiest ways to help the planet.

Turn off the tap

• While brushing your teeth
• While washing dishes
This simple habit saves a huge amount of water daily and protects our resources.
